Nusic Privacy Policy

Nusic is aware of the importance of the protection of privacy. The following paragraphs state in detail what personal data is used by nusic. Please read carefully. By using the services of the nusic application you acknowledge this Privacy Policy.

The most recent version of this document can always be found at Nusic's website.

What Personal Information Is Used?

App

Nusic uses MusicBrainz to find out about new releases on the user's device. It does so by sending the names of all the artists stored on the device to MusicBrainz.

Any albums returned by this query are in turn sent to the Cover Art Archive in order to display the album cover images.

The app does not send any other data.

Please see also MusicBrainz' privacy policy.

You can read more about Cover Art Archive's policy here.

Nusic does not store any data outside the user's device.

E-mail

Any incoming E-mails are stored on the servers of our E-mail provider. They are not shared with anyone.

Changes To The Privacy Policy

Nusic may ammend its Privacy Policy. The most recent version can be found on the website, as stated in the starting paragraph.

Questions?

If you have any questions or concerns in regard to the Privacy Policy, please share them with us at android@schnatterer.info.
